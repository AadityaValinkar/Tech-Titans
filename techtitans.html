About Our Program

Our Career Counselling and Guidance Programs are designed to help students explore their interests, understand the job market, and make informed decisions about their future careers. We provide personalized guidance to help students align their skills, strengths, and passions with their career paths.

Our Services

Career Assessment

We use scientifically validated tools to assess a student's aptitude, interests, and personality to suggest suitable career options.

Personalized Counselling

One-on-one counselling sessions with experienced career advisors to help students explore various career paths.

Workshops & Seminars

Interactive workshops and seminars to enhance awareness of various career opportunities and skills required in the job market.
